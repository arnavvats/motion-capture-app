The Face Recognition Pong Game

This game is built with OpenCV.js and Angular 8 cli.

Tap start and control the bar using your head movement.

You can also see your live video feed and position of your head. You dont need to move your head a lot, small movements should do fine.

This face recognition project runs entirely in browser and shows the power of moder javascript.
